# Controlling Traffic via 7Ds & The POEM Loop

## Shopee Key Examples

Next, let's look at key examples from Shopee. Shopee Live reaches around **200 million views**, and AI chat systems help handle delivery questions and improve click-through rates. During campaigns like **11.11**, flash sales are used to quickly boost traffic and conversions.

---

## The 7Ds Framework

From this, we can explain the **7Ds framework**. Digital businesses must balance:

- **Always-on engagement** — for daily interaction
- **Campaign-based activities** — for short-term spikes

However, this may lead to **subsidy-driven loyalty**, where users primarily react to discounts rather than long-term value.

---

## Mobile-First & The POEM Loop

Shopee is also strongly **mobile-first**, with around **95% of transactions on smartphones**. This creates a continuous traffic loop:

### Paid Media
The platform uses **Paid Media** like TikTok ads and KOLs to attract users into its app as Owned Media.

### Owned Media
Shopee's app acts as the primary **Owned Media** hub — capturing and retaining user traffic driven in from paid channels.

### Earned Media
After purchase, users generate reviews through **Shopee Coins**, creating **Earned Media**, which feeds back into the system at very low cost.

---

## Summary

Overall, this illustrates the **POEM framework** as an integrated loop where media channels reinforce each other and reduce acquisition cost.

| Media Type | Channel | Role |
|---|---|---|
| **Paid** | TikTok Ads, KOLs | Attract new users |
| **Owned** | Shopee App | Retain & convert users |
| **Earned** | Shopee Coins Reviews | Organic re-engagement at low cost |
