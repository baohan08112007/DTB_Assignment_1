# Touchpoint Classification & Lifecycle Marketing Strategy

---

## 1. Touchpoint Classification

### Paid Touchpoint
**Detail:** Sponsored posts on TikTok/Social media to capture traffic.

### Owned Touchpoint
**Detail:** Shopee App interface to drive direct interaction.

### Earned Touchpoint
**Detail:** Community forums and user reviews to build trust.

---

## 2. Lifecycle Marketing Strategy

| Stage | Touchpoint Type | Strategy |
|---|---|---|
| **Acquisition** | Paid Touchpoint | Driven by Paid Touchpoints to generate traffic. |
| **Conversion** | Owned Touchpoint | Triggered by Owned Touchpoints via vouchers/incentives. |
| **Retention & Advocacy** | Earned Touchpoint | Reinforced by Earned Touchpoints through review incentives. |

### Acquisition
Driven by Paid Touchpoints to generate traffic.

### Conversion
Triggered by Owned Touchpoints via vouchers/incentives.

### Retention & Advocacy
Reinforced by Earned Touchpoints through review incentives.
